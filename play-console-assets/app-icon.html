Two Dots — app icon export

How to export: click each button below. Two PNG files (icon.png and adaptive-icon.png) will save to your Downloads folder. Move both to two-dots/assets/, overwriting the existing blank placeholders. Then trigger a new EAS build to verify the icons render.

icon.png — iOS & Android non-adaptive fallback (solid dark bg)

adaptive-icon.png — Android adaptive foreground (transparent bg)
